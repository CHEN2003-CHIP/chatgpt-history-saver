# Contact Safety / 联系方式安全建议

## Is it safe to publish your real email? / 公开真实邮箱安全吗？

It is usually acceptable to publish an email address for extension support, but it is safer not to expose your primary personal mailbox.
公开一个用于插件支持的邮箱通常是可行的，但更安全的做法是不要直接暴露你的主个人邮箱。

## Recommended approach / 推荐做法

- Use a dedicated support mailbox for the extension
- 为插件单独准备一个支持邮箱
- Use a custom domain email if possible
- 如果可以，优先使用自定义域名邮箱
- Or use a separate mailbox dedicated to public contact only
- 或使用一个只用于公开联系的独立邮箱

## Avoid / 尽量避免

- Publishing your most sensitive daily-use personal email
- 公开你最常用、最敏感的私人邮箱
- Using an email tied to banking, identity verification, or important personal accounts
- 使用与你的金融、实名验证或重要私人账号绑定的邮箱

## Suggested public email patterns / 建议的公开邮箱形式

- `support@your-domain.com`
- `hello@your-domain.com`
- `chatgpt.history.saver@gmail.com`
- `chatgpt.history.saver@outlook.com`

## Practical recommendation / 实用建议

If you want to launch quickly, create a dedicated support mailbox just for this extension and use that in the privacy policy and store listing.
如果你想尽快上架，最实用的做法是专门为这个插件注册一个支持邮箱，并把它写进隐私政策和商店资料。
